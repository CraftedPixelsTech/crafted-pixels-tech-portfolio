GRADUATION PROJECT

Display

This product display project showcases a beautifully curated space, likely intended for a baby or nursery environment. The display features a combination of handmade and thoughtfully selected items that create a cozy, natural, and aesthetically pleasing environment.

    Macrame and Woven Art
  • The walls are adorned with macrame wall hangings, showcasing intricate weaving techniques. These pieces add a touch of bohemian elegance and contribute to the overall artisanal feel of the display.
    • Pastel Colors and Soft Textures
  • The color palette primarily consists of soft pastel tones, with a focus on light purples, greens, and natural shades. These colors are calming and ideal for a nursery setting. The soft textures in the display, including woven baskets and fabric-covered cushions, enhance the comfort and warmth of the space.
    • Handcrafted Crib and Canopy
  • A standout piece is the handcrafted crib, which is complemented by a light, airy canopy in a lavender hue. The crib is decorated with intricate weaving, adding a personal touch to the piece. This combination creates a dreamy, serene sleeping area for a baby.
    • Accessories and Decor
  • The display includes various accessories such as a stuffed teddy bear, hanging mobiles, and small baskets. These elements add charm and practicality to the space, making it both beautiful and functional.
    • Sustainability and Natural Materials
  • The use of natural materials like wood, cotton, and wicker throughout the display reflects a commitment to sustainability. This approach not only appeals to eco-conscious consumers but also enhances the organic and rustic feel of the design.
    • Interactive and Educational Features
  • A small table with books and educational materials suggests that the space is not just for rest but also for learning and play. This inclusion highlights the importance of creating a nurturing environment that supports a child’s development.
Overall Impression

This project successfully combines artistic craftsmanship with practical design, creating a space that is both beautiful and functional. It reflects a strong attention to detail and a deep understanding of what makes a nursery both welcoming and supportive for a child's early years. The emphasis on natural materials, soft colors, and handmade items creates a nurturing and peaceful environment, perfect for a baby’s room.
